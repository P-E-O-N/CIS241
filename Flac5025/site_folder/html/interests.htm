Ferrets:


They are amazing little critters that can live 6 – 10 years. They are very inquisitive and intelligent and make good pets if you can provide a safe environment for them. We currently have two ferrets named Whiskey and Widget. They can sleep up to 18 hours a day and spend the remainder in short bursts of activity, going crazy or eating snacks. I believe it’s a good idea to have at least 2 ferrets because they love to snuggle, and it gives them a playmate for the times where they are sprinting around having fun. They are smart enough to open drawers and love to steal things. If there is a safe dark corner to hide stuff in it’s only a matter of time before it’s full of whatever they have grabbed. Car keys, cat toys, socks etc. My Daughter absolutely loves them and plays with them every day.

Outdoors:


I love spending time outdoors. Leavenworth was such a pretty place to grow up as a kid that I think I just learned to appreciate the multitude of outdoor activities I had access to. I remember spending a lot of time mountain biking and swimming in the river and even cross-country skiing in the winter. I still enjoy snorkeling a ton and have been to the Crystal River in FL to snorkel with manatees and this summer we traveled to the Key West. If we can make time, we usually get to go on a couple of family camping trips in the north Cascades as well.
