A little bit about me:


I was born in San Bernardino CA and moved to WA when I was eight years old. My father was tired of living in southern CA and threw his tent in the car and took a two-week road trip north, stopping whenever he found someplace interesting. Eventually he ended up in Leavenworth WA and he always told me that as soon as he saw it he knew. Leavenworth is a beautiful little Bavarian village nestled in the Cascade mountains and only about 2 hours east of Seattle. I went to the local high school (Cascade) and grew up enjoying the gorgeous outdoor setting of the area. I was able to enjoy mountain biking, hiking, swimming, river rafting, skiing and backpacking in the Enchantments and Snow Lakes area. I went to the University of Washington after high school where I studied geology. After attending the UW, I worked a few odd jobs. Everything, from construction to the front office at Edward Jones, and eventually ended up working as a valet at Anthony's restaurant.



In 2006 I married my high school sweetheart who had also attended the UW with me. We have a beautiful 9-year old daughter named Quinn, along with our dog Sumo, a cat named Mims and two ferrets named Whiskey & Widget. Animals are PART of our family. If you have never had any experience with ferrets, they can be a lot of work (like having a two-year old) but are amazing pets. Those bouncing balls of fur will give you kisses, bite your toes, give you more kisses and then fall asleep in your shoe.
